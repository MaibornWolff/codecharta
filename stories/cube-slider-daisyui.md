# Restyle Cube Slider with daisyUI

**As a** user of the visualization
**I want** the cube slider to use daisyUI styling
**So that** it looks and behaves consistently with the rest of the application

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] The cube slider is rebuilt using daisyUI (Tailwind) components instead of its current styling
- [ ] No Angular Material or custom one-off styles remain for this slider
- [ ] The slider matches the look and feel (colors, spacing, states) of other daisyUI controls in the app
- [ ] Existing behavior is preserved: value range, step, live update of the map, and bound state stay unchanged
- [ ] Hover, focus, active, and disabled states are styled and accessible (keyboard operable)
- [ ] Tests/snapshots are updated to reflect the new markup
